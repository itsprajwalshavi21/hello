About KLE Technological University

KLE Technological University (KLE Tech) has its roots in B. V. Bhoomaraddi College of Engineering and Technology, Hubli (BVB), one of the premier engineering institutions of Karnataka. The founding organization KLE Society, Belgaum, established BVB college in 1947 with an aspiration of creating an institution that would lay the foundation of modern engineering education in northern region of Karnataka.

Infrastructure

To cater to modern engineering education needs, and support research activities, SoCSE is well equipped with state-of-art infrastructure.

Experimental Learning

SoCSE believes in educating students through learning by doing. Theoretical learning in the courses is enhanced by course projects and integrated projects.

Student Clubs/Chapters

SoCSE has student clubs like CodeChef, GeeksforGeeks which encourage competitive programming and building a community of competitive programmers.
